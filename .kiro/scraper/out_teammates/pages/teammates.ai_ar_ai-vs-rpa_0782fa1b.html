How to Choose for Your Industry

Choose AI Agents When:

  • Customer interactions require understanding context and nuance
  • Processes involve unstructured data like emails or documents
  • Decision-making requires analysis of multiple variables
  • You need 24/7 intelligent customer-facing support

Choose RPA When:

  • Tasks are highly structured with clear, unchanging rules
  • Working with standardized forms and data entry
  • Integration with legacy systems without APIs
  • High-volume, repetitive back-office processes

Quick Decision Framework

Choose AI Agents If:

  • You need customer-facing automation
  • You want fast deployment (days, not months)
  • You handle unstructured data
  • You need intelligent decision-making

Consider RPA If:

  • You have highly structured processes
  • You need legacy system integration
  • You have high-volume data entry
  • Processes rarely change
