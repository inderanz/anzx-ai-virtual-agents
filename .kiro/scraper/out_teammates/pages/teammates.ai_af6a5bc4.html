Frequently Asked Questions

Wait, this actually works without human training or oversight?

Yes - Raya resolves 78% of support tickets autonomously, Sara conducts complete interviews with 92% candidate satisfaction, and Rashed qualifies leads end-to-end. They make real decisions, not just follow scripts. When they encounter edge cases, they escalate with full context to your team.

What's the difference between AI teammates and chatbots or AI bots?

There are 5 levels of AI autonomy. Most companies use basic chatbots (level 1) for simple Q&A, or AI assistants like ChatGPT (level 2) that need constant prompting. AI teammates operate at level 5 - they're actually networks of specialized agents that make decisions, execute complex workflows, and operate autonomously with minimal human intervention. Think less 'bot that answers questions' and more 'digital colleague that gets work done.'

How is this different from ChatGPT or other AI tools?

ChatGPT responds to prompts - our AI teammates make independent decisions and handle complex workflows autonomously. They understand cultural nuances across 50+ languages, integrate natively with your business systems, and learn continuously from every interaction. While ChatGPT assists, our teammates truly collaborate as autonomous workforce members with enterprise-grade reliability and compliance.

How do AI teammates transform my team's capabilities?

Your team becomes strategic orchestrators instead of task executors. One person can now handle what used to require 10. Sales reps close bigger deals while AI qualifies leads. Support agents solve complex issues while AI handles FAQs. Recruiters build relationships while AI screens candidates. Everyone levels up to superhuman productivity.

Seriously, 10 minutes to go live? What's the catch?

No catch - OAuth connects your tools instantly, our models are pre-trained, and basic workflows activate immediately. Start with simple tasks, then customize advanced workflows over your first week.

Is my data secure? What about compliance?

SOC 2 Type II certified, GDPR and CCPA compliant. All data encrypted at rest and in transit. Your AI teammates never store sensitive information like passwords or payment details. Enterprise customers get dedicated instances and data residency options.

How quickly can AI teammates adapt to my unique business?

Instantly for basic tasks, 48 hours for complex workflows. Upload your playbooks, the AI learns your voice, connects to your tools, and starts delivering value immediately.
